What is secobap

secobap is a web-based fitness companion design to help users track their workout and manage their nutritional intake. This project aims to provide a simple yet effective tool for individuals on their fitness journey. Help tracking exercise and counting rest time for better manipulate gains and workout create for the users a clear path of what user have done and create a effective for them self by their own data

Why you should digitize your food and exercise data

The variety of race, gender, age and trainning style makes predicting the success of your trainning impossible. One way to overcome that is to study yourself, learn from your data,and find whats best for you.You can do this by digitizing your food intake, workouts, and even your sleep, mood, and menstual cycle(if applicable). Become your own scientist and learn about your potential!
